The Problem

Challenges They Faced

The client struggled with manual inventory tracking, delayed reporting, and disconnected systems that led to inefficiencies, stockouts, and poor customer experiences.

  • Manual inventory management causing errors and data duplication
  • Delayed reporting and lack of real-time insights
  • Disconnected POS and accounting systems
  • Poor stock visibility leading to overstocking and stockouts
  • Inefficient order processing and longer fulfillment time
!

Our Solution

What We Did

We developed a custom retail management system that automates inventory, connects all operations, and provides real-time insights through a unified dashboard.

  • Built a centralized inventory management system
  • Integrated POS, accounting, and reporting modules
  • Implemented real-time analytics and dashboards
  • Automated order processing and low-stock alerts
  • Provided role-based access and activity tracking
